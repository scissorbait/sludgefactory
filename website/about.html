About

This is TYOMA of TYOMA.COOL, reporting in!

Anyone who knows me knows that music runs through my veins, so I figured it'd be fun to setup a site just for me to share my love for sounds and noises.

While I try to keep my main site for complete, fully thought-out stuff, this is gonna just be like a music notebook. For the most part I intend to just share playlists and my listening habits, but who knows, I might add more stuff (like guitar/bass tabs) as it strikes my fancy.

  • My personal music collection is stored on a home media server running Plex.
  • Desktop/PC: I listen to my music through MusicBee and bulk edit metadata using MusicBrainz Picard.
  • Android: I used to use Poweramp, but ended up switching to Symfonium because it has streaming server support.
  • If I HAVE to stream, I prefer to use Youtube Music through this desktop app and a patched Android app.
  • I also have a huge CD collection, which I'm slowly expanding to include cassettes and vinyls.
  • My preferred headphone is the Sennheiser HD 599, but I like the Audio-Technica ATH-M50xBT2 for its portability as well.
  • I play physical copies of music using my LoopTone 10-in-1 multifunctional record player. I also use it as a general-purpose speaker and FM radio.
  • I've got a FiiO BTR3K... somewhere. Ashamed to admit I lost it, but I loved that thing. Also have a FiiO M9, which I want to get rid of.

Some of my favorite genres include grunge, jazz rap, doom metal, psychedelic rock/metal, stoner rock/metal, trip hop, drone doom, 70s R&B, new wave, boom bap, and progressive soul. I also like electronic music, but I'm unfamiliar with genres outside of UK garage and 90s jungle. I listen to other stuff too.

The only genres I actively dislike are hyperpop, black metal, and country pop. You will not convince me to like or even listen to them.
