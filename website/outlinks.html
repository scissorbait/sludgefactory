Other sites

A collection of other music-focused sites that I peruse from time-to-time.

Lethal Compound - Homepage of a music enjoyer filled with their playlists, gig writeups, and more!

Free Music Archive -Repository of copyright-friendly, free-to-use music made by independent artists.

Soulseek - P2P file-sharing program that you can use to download and share music with other users.

Robert Christgau - Homepage of an American rock critic. I don't agree with all his takes, but I like to read his reviews now and again.

inter.tube (paid) - "Music locker" that you can store your music files in and stream over the Subsonic protocol.

MusicBee - Fully-featured and customizable music player/manager, my favorite in fact.

Radiooooo - "Musical time machine" where you can explore music by country, decade, and energy level.

Gnoosic - Tool that recommends artists you might like based on three that you already like.

Music-Map - Tool that takes an artist you input and creates a big map of similar artists.

Listen to Wikipedia - Plays a tone for every new edit in Wikipedia's "Recent changes" feed.

tane.us - Listen to hourly Animal Crossing music based on the current time.

paper cd case - Generates a foldable paper CD case based on info you put in. Also makes jewel case inserts.

Ichigo's Sheet Music - Ancient, still-active archive of sheet music for various game and anime soundtracks
