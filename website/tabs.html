Guitar tabs

I've been playing guitar since I was in middle school and despite all those years of practice, I still don't do much other than look up and play tabs for songs I like. However, some tabs for certain songs really suck, are written for the wrong tuning, or are completely nonexistent. Here I'll share some tabs I wrote all by myself for your viewing pleasure.

I don't aim for 100% accuracy with these but I try to get pretty close or at least good enough for it to sound right. If you have any corrections you can email me and I'll take a look.
